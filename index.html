What are the signs of gum disease?

+

Look for these signs:

  • Red, swollen, or tender gums
  • Bleeding gums while brushing or flossing
  • Bad breath
  • Loose teeth

Is it safe to use herbal remedies for toothache?

+

While some herbal remedies may offer temporary relief, it's crucial to consult a dentist for proper diagnosis and treatment.

What are the best ways to prevent cavities in children?

+

  • Regular brushing and flossing
  • Limiting sugary drinks and snacks
  • Regular dental checkups
  • Fluoride treatments

How often should I replace my toothbrush?

+

It's recommended to replace your toothbrush every 3-4 months or sooner if the bristles are frayed.

What are the benefits of dental sealants?

+

Dental sealants create a protective barrier over the grooves of teeth, preventing food particles and bacteria from getting trapped and causing cavities.

Do I need to see a dentist if I have no pain?

+

Yes, regular dental checkups are essential even if you don't experience any pain. Early detection and prevention are key to maintaining good oral health.

What are the risks of smoking on oral health?

+

Smoking increases the risk of gum disease, tooth loss, oral cancer, and other serious health problems.
